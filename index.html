jadrien roi - aspiring front end web developer

Hi! I'm Jadrien Roi Aguilar, a passionate and motivated BSIT student from Arellano University, currently based in Manila, Philippines. I specialize in front-end web development, with hands-on experience using HTML, CSS, and Tailwind CSS. Most of my projects so far are for school and personal practice, helping me sharpen both my technical skills and creative approach to clean, user-friendly design.

Whether it's developing a responsive website or learning a new chord, I bring the same level of focus and enthusiasm to everything I do.
